With over a decade of experience as a GIS specialist, I've worked on diverse projects spanning natural resource management, conservation planning, and geospatial storytelling. I've created dynamic maps and analyses using tools like ArcGIS Pro, Python, and Adobe Illustrator, tackling challenges such as invasive species mapping, green infrastructure planning, and connectivity analysis. My career highlights include developing public-facing ArcGIS StoryMaps, managing field data collection systems, and developing metrics to assess conservation success. Currently, I'm advancing my skills through a Master of Geospatial Data Science at the University of Canterbury, blending technical expertise with innovative solutions to address complex environmental and spatial challenges.
